About Us

Based in North Sentinel Island our delicious Italian dishes give you a taste of what centuries of culinary excellence have created. From pasta to pizza to sweets-a to the streets-a. We've got a little bit of everything to satisfy your hungry tummy.

Our founder and CEO, Ronald McDonald built this business off the idea "When life gives you lemons, squeeze them onto some linguine". That's why our restaurant proudly offers complimentary lemons with your linguine. Just ask!
